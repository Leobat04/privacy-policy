Poker Terminal — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

Poker Terminal is developed by Leo G. ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, or location.

Camera Usage

Poker Terminal requests access to your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning poker chips using AI image recognition. Photos taken within the app are transmitted to our secure server for analysis only and are immediately discarded after processing. We do not store, share, retain, or use any images or camera data for any purpose other than returning chip count results to your device.

In-App Purchases

The app offers consumable in-app purchases (session tokens) processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not access, process, or store any payment or billing information. All transactions are handled exclusively by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only using iOS secure storage (Keychain and UserDefaults):

  • Token balance
  • Session state
  • Poker session statistics

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Third Party Services

The app uses the following third party services:

  • Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI chip image analysis. Images are transmitted solely for analysis and are not stored or retained.

GDPR — European and Swiss Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access any personal data held about you
  • The right to erasure of your personal data
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, these rights are satisfied by default. For any questions or requests please contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Poker Terminal is intended for users aged 17 and older in accordance with its App Store age rating. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 17.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our server is encrypted using HTTPS. Token balance and session data stored on your device is protected using iOS Keychain secure storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
